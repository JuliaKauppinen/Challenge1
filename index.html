Recommendation

Water supply

Recommendation

Your water supply is on 83% of the 100% measured before the departure. At this rate you’ll have a spare 22% of the supply at the time of your arrival. Advise the people on board to take 5 minute showers instead of 10 minutes, so that you can continue using the water at your destination.

Oxygen level
